Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of the favorite food here is the reciepe for deliciously rich scrambled egg.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt Butter in a frying pan over a medium heat.
  2. Gently mix the egg and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the every 20 seconds (as if we are making an omlette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (It will continue to cook on the plate until served.)
